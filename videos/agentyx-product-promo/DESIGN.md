# Design System

## Overview

Agentyx is a light, precise B2B operating-system experience for AI employees. The website uses large centered product statements, pale blue-gray surfaces, thin borders, bright blue action states, and dashboard UI panels as proof. The visual personality is technical and executive: calm whitespace, crisp system labels, tabular metrics, and floating operational cards that show work moving through business systems.

## Colors

- **Primary Surface**: `#F7F9FC` - page background and video base.
- **Raised Surface**: `#FFFFFF` - dashboard cards and floating panels.
- **Primary Text**: `#172033` - large headings and brand text.
- **Dark Text**: `#111827` - secondary headline emphasis.
- **Muted Text**: `#475569` - descriptions and interface labels.
- **Primary Blue**: `#2563EB` - CTAs, hero emphasis, active states.
- **Strong Blue**: `#1D4ED8` - deeper button and glow accent.
- **Soft Blue**: `#3B82F6` - active UI badges and highlights.
- **Cyan Accent**: `#22D3EE` - system labels, paths, automation signals.
- **Success Green**: `#34D399` - completed workflow states.
- **Border**: `#D2DAE4` - thin UI separation.

## Typography

- **Display / Brand**: Plus Jakarta Sans, weights 600-800. Large, tight, low-drama headings.
- **Interface Body**: Inter, weights 300-700. Dashboard body copy, labels, card content.
- **System Mono**: JetBrains Mono, weights 400-500. Eyebrows, URLs, timestamps, status labels.
- **Video Scale**: hero lines 86-118px, section titles 58-82px, body 24-34px, UI labels 18-24px minimum.

## Elevation

Depth comes from thin blue-gray borders, pale blue fills, soft long shadows, and subtle cyan/blue glows. Product UI sits in framed browser-like panels with rounded corners and gently floating completion cards. Avoid heavy black shadows on light scenes; use quiet layered shadows and soft radial highlights.

## Components

- **Hero Statement**: centered two-line headline with blue final phrase.
- **Metric Row**: three compact KPI groups with simple line icons and mono labels.
- **Workspace Dashboard**: browser window with sidebar navigation, chat thread, workflow checklist, activity rail, and green completion result.
- **Floating Completion Cards**: tilted task cards stacked above the activity rail.
- **System Chips**: small rounded integration labels for ERP, CRM, WhatsApp, Email, Sheets, and documents.
- **Dark Command Panel**: deep navy operational dashboard from later sections; useful as a contrast moment.

## Do's and Don'ts

### Do's

- Use lots of clean negative space, then punctuate it with blue/cyan operational motion.
- Keep dashboards readable and structured with thin borders and card stacks.
- Use JetBrains Mono for system labels, URLs, timestamps, and status text.
- Animate proof through counters, progress lines, connected paths, and task completion states.
- Keep the Agentyx logo/text visible in the first and last beat.

### Don'ts

- Do not turn the promo into a dark neon AI cliché; the brand is mostly light and operational.
- Do not use decorative gradients as the main visual; use product UI and metrics.
- Do not make cards overly rounded; the site radius is restrained.
- Do not use generic stock people or a presenter; the chosen format is motion graphics.
- Do not shrink UI copy below readable video sizes.
