# Contax Brand Guidelines

Official guidelines for the **Contax** product brand. Contax is a specialized product brand under Lev Innovation.

## 1. Visual Identity
*   **Background**: `#09060f` — deep violet
*   **Foreground**: `#f5f3f7` — warm white
*   **Accent Color**: `#d946ef` (magenta) — used for indicators, highlights, and CTAs.

## 2. Integration & Usage
Contax inherits the typography and baseline spacing parameters from the core Agentyx Design System.

Import in presentations:
```html
<link rel="stylesheet" href="../../brand-systems/contax-brand-system/colors_and_type.css" />
```
